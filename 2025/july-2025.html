Maximum speed for Haymarket!

We've had a not insignificant team of Drupal experts working on the new What Car? website for Haymarket Media Group for several months, and we're delighted to announce Phase 1 was quietly and successfully launched last week. It features new and shiny things such things as the version reviews, allowing readers to drill into the specific version of a car they're interested in purchasing. There were immense challenges, changes to back-end APIs, migrations, all kinds of fun, so congratulations to the combined Haymarket and Code Enigma teams for pulling it off so smoothly.

Admins: checked your users lately?
If you're an administrator for your organisation's Code Enigma account, please do remember to check your users. We encourage you to check who has access to what at least annually, preferably quarterly. It's really easy, just:
  • login to our management dashboard
  • click on “Manage your users” on the right
  • use your phone app for two-factor authentication (if you're not set up yet you can do that here)
  • review who has access and what they can see
If you need any assistance, drop us a ticket as ever.

Rolling out Debian 12
We're delighted to announce we're well on top of rolling out Debian 12. We have done all of our test infrastructure, figured out all the gnarly bits, and we'll be in touch shortly to upgrade the fleet client-by-client. Debian 12 is the latest long-term stable (LTS) release of Debian Linux, which is the version of Linux we use for all your servers and containers, and will be supported until June 2028, as well as having all the latest software upgrades.
